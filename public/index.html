YelpCamp

Yelp Camp is a personal project that allows you to view campgrounds with a short description. Once you login or signup you can begin to create your own campgrounds that includes a title, image address and short description. You will also be able to leave a comment or edit a campground of yours. It was build with NodeJS and Express on back end and has Passport authorization. It is connected to a non-sql MongoDB database.
